# AI Vendor Risk Assessment

## Overview

This assessment evaluates security, privacy, compliance, and operational risks associated with third-party Artificial Intelligence (AI) service providers.

The objective is to establish a repeatable framework for evaluating AI vendors prior to enterprise adoption.

---

# Vendor Assessment Criteria

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Security | Security controls and protections |
| Privacy | Data handling and retention practices |
| Compliance | Regulatory and industry alignment |
| Reliability | Availability and resiliency |
| Governance | Transparency and accountability |
| Incident Response | Vendor response capabilities |

# Vendor Risk Questions

## Security

- Does the vendor support MFA?
- Is data encrypted in transit and at rest?
- Are security audits performed?

## Privacy

- Is customer data retained?
- Is customer data used for model training?
- Are privacy controls configurable?

## Compliance

- Does the vendor maintain SOC 2 certification?
- Does the vendor support GDPR requirements?
- Does the vendor support regulatory obligations?

## Incident Response

- Does the vendor provide incident notifications?
- Are response timelines defined?
- Is a breach communication process documented?

| Risk Level | Recommendation |
|----------|----------|

| Critical | Reject |

---

# Conclusion

Organizations should perform structured vendor risk assessments before adopting third-party AI services. Security, privacy, compliance, governance, and operational risks should be evaluated continuously throughout the vendor relationship lifecycle.
